Khronus aims to provide a descentralized infrastructure and tools for the convenient management of time in Smart Contracts

The Khronus Ecosystem

A fully descentralized network of nodes that provide automated calls to smart contracts.

A descentralized autonomus organization for the creation of free and open source tools designed for the management of time in Smart Contracts.

How Does it Work

For Client Contracts

  • Inherit from Khronus powered contract.
  • Register in the Khronus Coordinator and fund with Khron token.
  • Create your request.

For Clock Masters

  • Install Clock Master application in your infrastructure.
  • Deploy your Clock Master contract and register in the Khronus Coordinator.
  • Fund your Clock Master address and start serving alerts.
